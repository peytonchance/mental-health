You went to CAPS...now what?

A triage appointment at CAPS can result in any combination of the following:

  • Individual Brief Therapy at CAPS
  • Referral to a Local Therapist
  • Group Therapy
  • Couples Therapy
  • Pyschiatric Medication Evalution or Checkup
